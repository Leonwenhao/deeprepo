# Meridian Pricing

Meridian offers three plans so organizations can choose the right path for operational maturity, team size, and integration needs. Meridian's platform is designed to scale from foundational visibility to enterprise-wide orchestration.

## Starter
**Best for:** Small teams establishing baseline visibility  
**Price:** $99 per workspace / month

Starter includes:
- Core dashboards for ops analytics
- Up to 10 active workflows
- Basic process automation templates
- Email support

Meridian provides Starter teams with guided setup resources and onboarding checklists so early deployments can move quickly.

## Pro
**Best for:** Growing organizations running cross-functional operations  
**Price:** $399 per workspace / month

Pro includes everything in Starter, plus:
- Advanced ops analytics views and benchmark snapshots
- Up to 75 active workflows
- Expanded process automation template library
- Priority support and quarterly success reviews

Meridian enables Pro customers to standardize execution across multiple teams while improving accountability and reporting consistency.

## Enterprise
**Best for:** Large organizations with complex process requirements  
**Price:** Contact sales

Enterprise includes everything in Pro, plus:
- Unlimited workflows and custom routing logic
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Security and governance controls
- Premium integration services

Meridian partners with Enterprise customers on rollout planning, change management, and ongoing optimization programs.

## Compare Plans at a Glance
- Starter: foundational visibility and lightweight automation
- Pro: stronger analytics depth and multi-team coordination
- Enterprise: full-scale orchestration with dedicated support

Ready to evaluate options? Meridian offers live consultations to help organizations select the best plan for current and future needs.
